How to make Couscous - The Proper Way

A delicious and nutritious meal ideal for family gatherings.

Equipment needed:

  • Large pot
  • Large bowl
  • Steaming basket or steel collander
  • Large serving dish

Ingredients:

      For the meat and vegetable stew
    • 2tbsp olive oil
    • 500g lamb pieces
    • 2 small brown onions, finely chopped
    • Ras el hanout seasoning
    • 1tsp salt
    • 1 litre water
    • 2 small carrots, peeled and cut in half lengthwise
    • 1 courgette, diced
    • Handful of cooked chick peas
    • Small chilli
      For the couscous
    • 250g fine couscous (not instant or previously steamed)
    • 1tbsp olive oil
    • 1tbsp unsalted butter
    • 400ml water
    • Pinch of salt

Method:

  1. Heat the olive oil in the pot over medium heat, and lightly frying the meat until it is brown on all sides, for no more than 10 minutes. Remove it from the pot and set it aside.
  2. In the same pot, fry the onion until it is golden.
  3. Return the meat to the pot with the onions, and add the seasoning, salt and chilli. Add water and stir often for 20 minutes, then add the vegetable and chickpeas and let simmer for 40 minutes.
  4. While you wait for this, place the couscous in a large bowl and stir in the olive oil until every grain is evenly coated. Add the water a little at a time until it is fluffy.
  5. Put the couscous in the colander and on top of the stew to allow it to steam for around 15 minutes, then put it in the large bowl and fluff it out.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5, this time adding the butter while fluffing.
  7. Serve the couscous in a large bowl with the meat in the middle and decorate with the vegetables and sauce.
  8. Enjoy!
